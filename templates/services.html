What We Offer

Our Key Services

Stocker provides distinct features tailored for both traders and administrators.

Simulated Trading Platform (For Traders)

Access a curated list of mock stocks with simulated market movements. Practice buying and selling, manage your holdings, and track your performance in real-time with zero risk.

  • Real-time mock price tracking.
  • P&L calculation based on average buy price.
  • Detailed personal transaction history.

Comprehensive Portfolio Tracking (For Traders)

View your cash balance, total portfolio value, and equity at a glance. Our portfolio view provides detailed metrics for every holding.

  • View current market value vs. cost basis.
  • Instant cash balance updates after transactions.
  • Easy interface for liquidating positions (selling).

System Oversight & Management (For Admins)

Full administrative control over the trading environment, allowing for system-wide monitoring and management of users.

  • View all registered traders and their cash balances.
  • Monitor total market capitalization held by all users.
  • Access to the full ledger of all transactions.

Transaction Auditing (For Admins)

Maintain high transparency with access to the complete, immutable transaction ledger showing every buy and sell order executed on the platform.

  • Detailed records including user, stock, price, and timestamp.
  • Track total volume of buy and sell orders.
  • Ensure system integrity and track user activity.
